Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Introduction

At Raphael's Horizon, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through compliance with this policy. In this privacy policy, the words "we," "our," and "us" refer to Raphael's Horizon.

This policy describes the types of information we may collect from you or that you may provide when you visit our website, including https://raphaelshorizon.com and any other website owned and operated by us (collectively our "Website"), and our practices for collecting, using, maintaining, protecting, and disclosing that information.

Our Commitment to You

We are committed to maintaining the trust and confidence of our visitors. We want you to know that Raphael's Horizon is not in the business of selling, renting, or trading email lists with other companies and businesses for marketing purposes.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, including:

  • Name and contact information (email address, phone number, mailing address)
  • Demographic information
  • Payment information for donations or purchases
  • Communication preferences
  • Prayer requests and spiritual concerns

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our Website, we may automatically collect:

  • IP address and browser type
  • Pages visited and time spent on site
  • Referring website information
  • Device information and operating system
  • Clickstream data and browsing patterns

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our ministry services and spiritual guidance
  • Process your requests, prayers, and transactions
  • Send you updates, spiritual content, and event information
  • Improve our website and services to better serve our community
  • Communicate with you about ministry events and opportunities
  • Personalize your experience and provide relevant content
  • Ensure the security and integrity of our services

Spiritual Content and Communications

As a Christian ministry, we may use your contact information to send you spiritual encouragement, biblical teachings, and information about our ministry activities. You can opt-out of these communications at any time by using the unsubscribe link in our emails or contacting us directly.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties except in the following circumstances:

  • Trusted Service Providers: We may share information with trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our ministry, or servicing you, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.
  • Legal Requirements: We may release information when we believe release is appropriate to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect ours or others' rights, property, or safety.
  • Ministry Partners: With your consent, we may share prayer requests with our prayer team or ministry partners for intercessory prayer.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. These measures include:

  • Secure servers and encrypted connections (SSL)
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
  • Secure data storage and transmission protocols

While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access and receive a copy of your personal information
  • Correct or update your personal information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Object to processing of your personal information
  • Request restriction of processing your personal information
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our website and hold certain information. Cookies are files with a small amount of data which may include an anonymous unique identifier.

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of our website.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
